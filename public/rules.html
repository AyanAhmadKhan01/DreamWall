Rules

These rules apply to all users of Dream Wall. Not following them may result in your uploads being removed or you losing the ability to create posts, upload, flag or tag. If you have questions about any of them, please ask a staff member or in the forum. Note that in a few cases there will be exceptions to these rules where staff will use their best judgment in the interest of the site.

Notice: These rules may be updated at any time.

  1. No Adult Content: Users are prohibited from uploading or sharing any content that contains explicit adult material, including nudity or sexual content.
  2. No Violent Content: Content that depicts violence, gore, or any form of physical harm is strictly prohibited.
  3. No Hate Speech or Discrimination: Any form of hate speech, discriminatory content, or content that promotes racism, sexism, homophobia, or any other form of bigotry is not allowed.
  4. No Propaganda or Extremist Content: Content that promotes extremist views, propaganda, or political agendas is forbidden.
  5. Respect Intellectual Property: Users must not upload content that infringes on the intellectual property rights of others, including copyrighted images and trademarks.
  6. No Spam or Unauthorized Advertising: Posting spam, irrelevant links, or unauthorized advertisements is not permitted.
  7. Respect Privacy: Sharing personal information or private content of others without their explicit consent is prohibited.
  8. No Malware or Phishing: Uploading or linking to content that contains malware, phishing attempts, or any other malicious software is strictly forbidden.
  9. Maintain Community Respect: Users must treat others with respect and courtesy. Harassment, bullying, or abusive behavior will not be tolerated.
  10. No Misinformation: Sharing false information, fake news, or misleading content is prohibited.
